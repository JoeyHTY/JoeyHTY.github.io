About Me

Personal Background

Hi! My name is Ting Yi He, and I come from the vibrant city of Chongqing, China. My fascination with the cosmos began at a young age, inspired by the STARWARS movie series. Although I initially considered Aerospace Engineering due to my interest, my passion for exploring the unknown and the thrill I experienced when independently deriving theoretical formulas led me to pursue astrophysics. This curiosity and dedication directed me to explore various scientific disciplines, ultimately solidifying my passion for astrophysics. Outside of academics, I enjoy playing badminton and basketball, and I have a deep appreciation for jazz music, especially jazz piano.
